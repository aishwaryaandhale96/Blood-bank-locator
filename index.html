Why Should You Donate Blood?

Blood donations are essential for surgeries, cancer treatment, chronic illnesses, and traumatic injuries. Whether it's whole blood, platelets, or plasma, your donation is crucial for patients in need.

Facts About Blood Donation:

  • A single donation can save up to three lives.
  • About 6.8 million people donate blood every year.
  • There’s no substitute for human blood; it can’t be manufactured.
